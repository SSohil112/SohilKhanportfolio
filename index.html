Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked

All your common questions — answered in one place.

01Can I get a free sample / demo edit to see your quality?
Absolutely! I'm happy to do a small demo sample edit so you can experience my editing style and quality firsthand. It's a great way to see if we're a good fit before moving to a full project. Just reach out and we'll work out the details.
02How long does it take to edit one video?
It depends on the length and complexity of the project. A typical Instagram Reel (30–60 sec) is delivered within 1–2 days. For a YouTube video or short film, expect 3–7 days. Rush delivery is also available — just reach out and we'll figure it out.
03What makes PrimeCuts edits different from others?
It's all in the details. We focus on narrative precision rather than just generic cuts. Every frame is motivated by the beat, every color grade is designed to evoke specific emotions, and every sound effect is placed to create an immersive experience. We deliver high-fidelity 4K content that is optimized for engagement.
04How do I share raw footage? How will I receive the final file?
You can share footage via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or Dropbox. The final edited video is delivered through the same platforms in 1080p or 4K. Reels are delivered in vertical format (9:16) by default.
05Are revisions included? How many times can I request changes?
Yes! Every project includes unlimited free revisions. Your complete satisfaction is my priority. Additional revisions beyond that are available at a reasonable cost.
06How does payment work? Is an advance required?
I accept payments via UPI, bank transfer, or PayPal. For new clients, a 50% advance is required upfront with the remaining 50% due on delivery. Flexible monthly packages are also available for long-term collaborations.
